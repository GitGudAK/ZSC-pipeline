THE SKY GARDEN

High above the clouds, a floating island holds the last garden in the world. Petals drift on warm updrafts, catching golden sunlight.

A young girl named Sora tends to the flowers. She wears a white dress with leaf patterns. Her hair is the color of cherry blossoms.

She notices a withering rose at the edge of the island. Kneeling down, she cups her hands around it and breathes softly. A gentle green glow emanates from her palms.

The rose blooms again, its petals unfurling in brilliant crimson. Sora smiles. But as she looks up, she sees dark storm clouds approaching from the horizon.

She stands and walks to the edge of the floating island, gazing down at the ruined world below — cracked earth and dead forests stretching endlessly. A single tear rolls down her cheek as wind whips through the garden.
