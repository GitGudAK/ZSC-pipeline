THE LAST TRAIN

A solitary train glides through an endless twilight landscape. The sky is painted in deep purple and orange.

Inside, a boy named Ren sits alone in the last car, staring out the window. He holds a crumpled photograph of a smiling girl.

The train passes through a tunnel of cherry blossom trees, pink petals swirling past the windows like snow. Ren presses his hand against the glass.

As the train emerges, a ghostly girl in a school uniform appears in the seat across from him. She smiles gently. Ren's eyes widen with recognition.

He reaches out toward her, but she begins to dissolve into particles of light. The particles float out through the window and rise into the twilight sky, becoming new stars.

Ren watches them go, a bittersweet smile on his face. The train continues onward into the endless dusk.
