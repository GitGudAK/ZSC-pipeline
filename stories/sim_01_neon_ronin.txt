NEON RONIN

In the sprawling cyberpunk district of Neo-Akihabara, a lone samurai named Kenji walks through rain-soaked neon streets. His katana glows with embedded circuitry.

He stops before a massive holographic billboard advertising "Paradise Corp." His reflection shimmers in a puddle below.

A drone swarm descends from above. Kenji draws his blade in one fluid motion, the circuit patterns igniting blue.

He cuts through three drones in a spinning arc, sparks flying against the rain. The remaining drones retreat into the fog.

Kenji sheathes his sword and continues walking. The city towers loom overhead, their lights flickering like dying stars. He disappears into an alleyway, coat billowing behind him.
