In a world where stars are dying, Hikari, a young girl with silver-white hair, discovers a fading star in an abandoned alleyway. She reaches out, her hand glowing with a soft blue magical energy. As her fingertips touch the celestial remnant, the star suddenly ignites, illuminating the dark alley with blinding light.

However, the moment the star is reborn, a wave of pain hits her. Yoru, a mysterious boy in a dark coat, steps from the shadows. "You shouldn't have done that," he says coldly, his mismatched eyes gleaming. Hikari looks at him, confused. "Who are you?" she asks, the memory of her own purpose already beginning to slip away.
